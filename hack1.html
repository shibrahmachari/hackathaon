ABOUT US

This initiative aims to reduce waste production in the country through effective means for maximum resource utilisation,
thus reducing stress on alarmingly depleting resources and rising pollution.This portal expedites the entire process of waste
segregation with minimum efforts required from both parties. Dumping all kinds of waste products together has been
an issue of concern and a major challenge for environmentalists. We've come together to take the first step towards a cleaner India.
